## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Login with any username/password
3. Enjoy the welcome page
4. After 10 seconds, auto-redirect to Bible verse page
5. Click "See Today's Verse" to view Tamil Bible verses
6. Tamil voice-over will play automatically
